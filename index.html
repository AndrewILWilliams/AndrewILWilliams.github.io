Hi there! I'm a postdoctoral research associate at Princeton University and NOAA's Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory. I got my PhD in 2023 from the Department of Atmospheric, Oceanic and Planetary Physics at the University of Oxford.

My research focuses on understanding the role that clouds and large-scale circulations play in the climate system and its response to human activities. This is a broad Recent topics of interest include: tropical dynamics, radiative feedbacks, the hydrological cycle and aerosol radiative forcing.

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you would like to chat or collaborate! My email is: andrew.williams@noaa.gov
