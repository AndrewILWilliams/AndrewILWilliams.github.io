Research Group

Andrew Williams is an Assistant Professor of Climate Science at Columbia University. His group studies tropical convection, radiative transfer, and the large-scale circulation of the atmosphere and ocean, using theory and a hierarchy of numerical models. He likes to hang out with his cat.  [CV]
